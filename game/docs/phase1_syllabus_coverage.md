# Phase 1 syllabus coverage

The Phase 1 Android MVP is intentionally narrow in chapter count and deep in coverage quality.

## Canonical source

- Chapter 8 PDF: `game/references/ncert/jemh108.pdf`
- Chapter 9 PDF: `game/references/ncert/jemh109.pdf`

## Coverage promise

- Every NCERT subsection in the two attached PDFs is represented.
- Every major exercise pattern is mapped to either a level or a mastery gate.
- Coverage is concept-complete, not question-by-question reproduction.
- The gameplay loop for every competency follows:
  - Build
  - Recall
  - Apply
  - Revise
  - Master

## World breakdown

1. `World 1 - Triangle Lab`
   Covers Chapter 8.1 and 8.2 with six levels.
2. `World 2 - Sacred Angles`
   Covers Chapter 8.3 with five levels.
3. `World 3 - Identity Forge`
   Covers Chapter 8.4 with five levels.
4. `World 4 - Height Seeker`
   Covers Chapter 9.1 with six levels.

## Assessment model

- Each world ends in a mastery gate level.
- Learners can retry indefinitely.
- Repeated mistakes push targeted prompts into the revision queue.
- Hints reveal setup only and never show the full working or final answer.

## Validation

The structural validator checks:

- source PDFs exist in the repo
- world and level counts match the plan
- template ids are limited to the approved six templates
- every competency tag is defined and used
- every level contains the full learning loop
- the coverage matrix references only real levels
